The site refresh that took place last year has paid off in dividends

Posted by Jam_Lopez on October 29, 2016

Design Your Garments
Shop from our seasonal collection or catalogue of luxury fabrics and customize them with our design options to create your ideal garments. Everything is proudly made in America.

Fill Out Your Profile
Here you can share as much information as you like, including details about your personal fit preferences and typical problem areas. Our tailoring team carefully reviews your responses and creates custom try-on garments for your initial fitting.

Initial Fitting
2-4 weeks after your first purchase you will receive your complimentary try-on garments. Log-in to your AoS account and follow our fitting instructions. Simply upload 4 photos in your try-on garments and share open-ended feedback about the fit. The try-ons are yours to keep - no returns, no hassle.

Expert Tailors Finalize Your Pattern
Our tailoring team carefully reviews your fitting photos and written feedback, makes full bespoke adjustments to your pattern, and crafts your final garment. While other “online tailors” reduce the tailoring experience to self-measuring and algorithms, we offer a personal relationship with your tailor and provide real, human expertise for every client.

Your Final Garments Arrive
4-6 weeks after your initial fitting, you will receive your final garments. They arrive hanging and pressed, so you can throw them on and enjoy the feeling of expert tailoring right out of the box. If it needs any final tweaks, we cover those too. We then save your perfected pattern, giving you access to one-click shopping with your new personal tailor - anytime, anywhere.
